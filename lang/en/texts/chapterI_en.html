Chapter I - Regret

Humen,
the furthest developed race on earth...

What follows are the Avienians, lords of the skies and conquerers of spiritual tranquility.

Even before I was brought onto this world,
they waged wars against eachother.

Land, Sky and Sea.
Ressources, Food and Manpower...
and worst of it all, Knowledge, intelligence and wisdom.
All of these were fought for - concepts that make our world...
or break it.

They hated eachother like no other.
But one day, they will be fighting side by side - against whom they shall call

Vodjes of Avienya...

Mere decades were left until he'll be reborn - guided by the
and learning of his terrible fate.

The sacrifices he has to make, to return to his former glory - a currency in the eyes of the gods.
His regret and love for his family keeping him from becoming what was once an ancient evil of man.

What once were his last words on the throne of madness, are now considered ancient rites of his people. "Nihil aliud." - "nothing else matters."

This was the story my mother always told me when it was time for me to sleep.

Part I - awaken thine memories.

My name is Lillim Dorothea.
Today, I'm only known as Lillim, the unwanted - Lillim, the Orphan.
I was once the only child of a war veteran and his beloved maiden - Their names were Friedrich van Avienya and Erenea van Avienya, fromerly known as Erenea Havenstedt.
They lived happy lifes before the war.
Father worked simply as an pharmacist and engineer, having also years of war experience on his back.
Mother mostly worked as a caretaker, though she stopped after she got pregnant.
My birth went on without consequence, brought onto the world as healthy as a child could have been.
My mother hasn't suffered from any sickness, even after months of taking care of me.
Though this luck didn't last long, not until another war broke out which would take my father away from me.
At first he tried to convince his superiors that his health had already deteriorated after the three wars he had fought before, they didn't listen.
Instead they forced him into the draft by telling him that "you either join, or your services and your family will suffer under your ruthless concideration."
He was stubborn, but finally budged - so that we will not be harmed.

They lied.

The first weeks of the war went unnoticed, going by like every other day.
but mother started to notice, that the child support and income slowly dwindled.
She thought it was just the war causing shortages, but she didn't know that the war hasn't even begun yet.
Months went on and our income got lower, so low, that she started to sell many of our belongings, some even unpriceable.
But it still wasn't enough.
Rents went higher and soon our financial stability depleted.
We were poor.
Poverty struck in just mere weeks...
and worst of it all is the war hasn't even begun yet.
It was months after the draft that the fighting finally began - my father in the front lines.
It was on my third birthday when I first asked of fathers condition,
Mother calmly told me "he's our hero, Lillim. He'll save us from this war and this poverty, I assure you."
Those of course were partial lies.
I did believe that he was a Hero.
A legend, to be certain.
But he alone couldn't win a war on a one to thirty scale.
It was us, Havenstedt, a small autonomous region, fighting against the might that is the Rosenkreuz imperium.
We were certainly doomed.
But demise struck earlier...

It was on the dawn of my fifth birthday when I was woken up by the sounds of war waging beyond our doors and windows.
They have arrived - so soon?
Mother grabbed me by the hand and we fled the house - only to be caught off guard by what has now been defined as a "tank".
Mother got wounded and captured by Soldiers who I first couldn't identify - She told me to run off and hide in the woods.
And so I did.
I managed to shake off the pursuers, but what will happen to mother?
I couldn't leave her to these monsters, I thought.
And so I tried to ran back, only to be caught by what seemed like a stray bullet.
It incapacitated me and barely entered my left lung.
And there I remained, With my back against the trees, all alone in the woods, crying.
"Mother" I choked, "please help me."
I was done for.
But it was like the Gods smiled upon me - I passed out and was left to expierence my own death.
I knew I would die,
I knew that I would be left all alone, only to wither away in some near forest.
But what I didn't know would come...
was the Scarlet witch.

There I was, death closing in, and the Scarlet Witch present.
She looked exactly like Mother had always told me - A tall, black haired Woman, wearing a mask of an ancient god-like being - Everything below her feet resembling a lakem of crimson.
My sorroundings were nothing more than a jet-black deep abyss.
Nothing but the Witch representing the crimson-red of blood.
She looked at me and closed in.
I was petrified.
She told me in an angelic voice "You are not to die in such place, Lillim.
You didn't deserve to have such filth kill you."
She keeled down and came closer to me patrified gaze, slowly embracing me with a comforting hug.
"You are just like me, destined to die by the hands of the Godslayer.
That's how we shall live, eternally being reborn to meet the same demise, Lillyemel.
And now...
Wake up and embrace the world to your heart's content, for you will live until eternity's end."
Since that day have I never felt the same anymore.
I wasn't the same anymore.
I was something... different.

Clapping.
"Klang liked story, so did Anna!"
Annalott nods and cheers.
"Thank you both!"
Those are Annalot and Klang.
I met Annalott after I have fled the Orphanage I was put in by the Goverment.
Klang however was a find - He flew all the way from Avienya to avoid the current civil war that is waged there.
We found him knocked out on our roof, presumeably after he collapsed from his stress and physical work.
Annalot is a Intelligent Germano-Avienian Human, who has taught me Latin and Avienian aside from cooking.
Klang, called after the Germano word for "Noise", was like a miracle, since we both had quite a depressed mood sorrounding us since we fled the Orphanage.
Klang, which is what he presumeably meant, is somewhat of an Crossbreed between a Human and an actual Avienian, which is quite an anomaly nowadays since the wars they've been continuesly waging.
Atleast we found a new home, a left and forgotten Cavern, in which we now reside.
I just wished for Mother and Father to be here...
